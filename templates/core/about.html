About MjengoLink

Building trust and connecting communities through quality construction services.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, MjengoLink emerged from a simple observation: the construction industry in Kenya, while rich with skilled artisans, lacked a reliable platform connecting them with homeowners in need of quality services.

We saw the challenges homeowners faced in finding trustworthy fundis and the difficulties artisans encountered in securing consistent, well-paying work. MjengoLink was born to bridge this gap.

Today, we're proud to be Kenya's fastest-growing construction marketplace, connecting thousands of verified artisans with homeowners across the country.

Our Mission & Values

Quality

Our Impact

Through our platform, we're creating economic opportunities while improving the quality of construction services across Kenya.
